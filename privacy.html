Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our automation tool ("the App") handles your information when you connect your TikTok account.

1. Information Access

The App requests access to your TikTok public profile and the ability to upload videos. This is used solely to automate synchronization from your other social media platforms.

2. Data Storage

We do not store your social media content on our servers. The App acts as a bridge to transfer content via official APIs.

3. Your Rights

You can revoke access to this application at any time through your TikTok account settings.
